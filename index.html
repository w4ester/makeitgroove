Cover Letter

May 13, 2025

Dear Hiring Committee,

I am writing to express my enthusiastic interest in the Elementary Instrumental Music Teacher position at Cedar Grove Elementary School. With more than two decades of experience inspiring young musicians in Anne Arundel County Public Schools, I bring a unique blend of instrumental expertise, cross‑curricular STEM innovation, and an unwavering commitment to nurturing students’ creativity and confidence.

Over the past 15 years at North County High School, I have:

  • Grown guitar‑ensemble enrollment from 12 to 48 students and founded jazz, percussion, and chamber groups.
  • Directed three seasonal concerts each year, welcoming 600+ family members and raising $4 k annually for program needs.
  • Led the Green Technology Pathway, designing a school garden used in cross‑disciplinary “sound‑in‑nature” lessons that earned the school Maryland Green School designation.

These experiences underscore my belief that music education thrives when it is hands‑on, community‑centered, and connected to the real world. I am eager to bring this philosophy to Cedar Grove’s elementary learners—introducing them to instruments, fostering ensemble collaboration, and integrating simple science concepts like sound waves through engaging, age‑appropriate activities.

I would welcome the opportunity to share a sample lesson, concert footage, or further discuss how my background aligns with your vision for a vibrant, inclusive music program.

Thank you for your time and consideration. I look forward to the possibility of working together.

Sincerely,
Michael O'Donnell
